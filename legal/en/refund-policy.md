# Refund Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-09

## 1. Scope

This policy applies to all paid Seizn subscriptions purchased through Litheon LLC (Wyoming, USA), including Author Memory Web (Track 1), API · MCP (Track 2), and Desktop (Track 3, when launched).

## 2. Annual subscriptions

You may request a full refund within **14 days** of the initial purchase. We will refund the full amount paid through Stripe; processing may take 5–10 business days depending on your bank.

After 14 days, annual subscriptions are non-refundable except where required by law (e.g., consumer protection statutes in your jurisdiction). You may cancel at any time, and your access continues until the end of the paid period.

## 3. Monthly subscriptions

Monthly subscriptions can be canceled at any time and remain active until the end of the current billing period. We do not pro-rate or refund the unused portion of the current month.

## 4. How to request a refund

Open Settings → Billing → Customer Portal and select **Cancel and refund**. The flow is fully self-service and processes immediately for eligible refunds. No human review is required for refunds inside the 14-day window.

If the self-service flow does not show your subscription, contact billing@seizn.com with your account email and the order reference; we respond within 3 business days.

## 5. Failed payments

If a renewal payment fails, your account remains active for a 7-day grace period while we retry the charge. After grace, the subscription downgrades to Free tier. No refund is owed for the failed charge.

## 6. Disputes and chargebacks

Please contact billing@seizn.com before initiating a chargeback with your card issuer. Chargebacks filed without prior contact may result in suspension of your account pending dispute resolution.

## 7. Charter pricing

Charter pricing is a discounted rate offered to early subscribers, locked until 2027-05-01. If you cancel and resubscribe after the Charter window closes, the standard regular rate applies. Refunds within the 14-day window do not affect Charter eligibility on a future subscription if you resubscribe within the Charter window.

## 8. Tax

Sales tax (US) and VAT (EU/UK/Korea) are calculated and collected by Stripe at checkout. Refunds include any tax originally paid; the tax refund is processed automatically by Stripe.

## 9. Contact

- Billing: billing@seizn.com
- General support: support@seizn.com
